Green Lagos Tours - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Green Lagos Tours

This name highlights our eco-friendly tour services focused on exploring natural, cultural, and historical attractions in Lagos, Nigeria. It's memorable, descriptive, and aligns with sustainability goals.

Optional domain: greenlagostours.com

Site Purpose

Green Lagos Tours will provide tourists and locals with curated information about eco-tours, cultural sites, natural reserves, and historical landmarks in Lagos. The site will include booking options, gallery sections, local guide bios, and blog articles promoting sustainable tourism.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a guided eco-tour around Lekki Conservation Centre?
  • Can I book a group tour and see reviews of past trips?

Color Schema

Primary Green (#008037): Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
Accent Gold (#FFD700): Used for highlights, links, and section dividers.

Typography

  • Montserrat – Used for all headings to create a bold, modern look.
  • Roboto – Used for body text for high readability and clean design.
